## Short Description (132 chars max)
Scan your Gmail for bills and due dates. AI-powered bill tracker with paid/unpaid checklist and daily reminders.

## Detailed Description
Never miss a bill again.

BillAlert scans your Gmail inbox for bill, invoice, and subscription emails and automatically extracts the payee, amount, and due date — all in one clean popup.

**Features:**
• 🔍 Smart Gmail scanning — finds bills, invoices, subscriptions, and payment reminders
• 🤖 AI-powered extraction — pulls out amounts and due dates automatically
• ✅ Paid/Unpaid tabs — track which bills you've paid with a persistent checklist
• 🔔 Daily reminders — set custom notification times so you never miss a due date
• 🔒 Privacy first — your email content never leaves your device; only subject lines and snippets are processed

**How it works:**
1. Sign in with your Google account
2. Click "Scan Now" to scan your inbox
3. See all your upcoming bills in one place
4. Mark bills as paid as you pay them
5. Set reminder times to get notified daily

**Privacy:**
BillAlert uses read-only Gmail access and never modifies your email. Full email bodies are never sent anywhere — only subject lines and short snippets are processed by AI to extract bill data. Your data stays on your device.
